What is Zakat?

Zakat is one of the Five Pillars of Islam and is a form of obligatory charity. It is calculated as 2.5% of your qualifying wealth that has been held for a full lunar year.

Who must pay Zakat?

  • Must be a Muslim
  • Must be of sound mind
  • Must have reached puberty
  • Must own wealth above the nisab threshold
  • Wealth must be held for a full lunar year
