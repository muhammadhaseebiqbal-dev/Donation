CHANGING LIVES AND BUILDING A BRIGHTER FUTURE

Al-Mustafa Welfare Trust is a Muslim charity dedicated to eradicating global poverty, and for over 40 years has been delivering life-saving humanitarian aid to the poorest countries in the world.

We transform millions of lives daily through healthcare and sight restoration. Pioneering free eye camps in Pakistan, our programme now spans Asia, Africa & the Middle East, restoring sight to 252,028 people.

Our projects also include Hifz education, orphan care, food aid, clean water, and disaster relief in Gaza, Yemen, Syria, Afghanistan, and Lebanon, fostering long-term change.
