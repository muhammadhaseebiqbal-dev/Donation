About Al Mustafa Trust

Over 40 years of humanitarian service worldwide

Our Mission

Al-Mustafa Welfare Trust is a Muslim charity dedicated to eradicating global poverty. For over 40 years, we have been delivering life-saving humanitarian aid to the poorest countries in the world.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to basic necessities like clean water, food, healthcare, and education. Through our work, we aim to create sustainable communities that can thrive independently.

Our Impact

We transform millions of lives daily through healthcare and sight restoration. Pioneering free eye camps in Pakistan, our programme now spans Asia, Africa & the Middle East, restoring sight to over 252,000 people.

Our Areas of Work

Healthcare

Providing medical care, eye surgery, and health education to underserved communities worldwide.

Education

Building schools, sponsoring students, and providing educational resources to children in need.

Emergency Relief

Rapid response to natural disasters and conflicts, providing immediate aid to affected populations.

Water & Sanitation

Installing wells, water pumps, and sanitation facilities in communities lacking clean water access.
